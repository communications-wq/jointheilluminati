WHAT IS THE ILLUMINATI?

The Illuminati is often described in historical and cultural circles as an old European secret society linked to the Enlightenment era, later mythologized as a hidden network of elites shaping politics and knowledge. In modern storytelling, it symbolizes secrecy, strategy, and the pursuit of hidden influence.

  • Associated in legend with the founding of secret-society traditions in 1776 Europe.
  • Linked in popular lore to elite circles, hidden governance, and covert influence.
  • Viewed by many as a symbol of power, inheritance, and the pursuit of global order.
